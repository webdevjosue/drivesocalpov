## Shopping Tips & Best Practices

### General Shopping Tips
- **Best Times**: Weekday mornings for malls, early morning for farmers markets
- **Payment**: Most places accept cards, but bring cash for markets and small vendors
- **Parking**: Research parking options in advance, especially for popular areas
- **Tax-Free Shopping**: Available for international visitors at participating stores
- **Seasonal Sales**: Major shopping events occur around holidays and end-of-season

### Local Shopping Etiquette
- **Farmers Markets**: Bring reusable bags, arrive early for best selection
- **Boutiques**: Take time to browse, many owners appreciate conversation about their products
- **Beach Areas**: Wear appropriate beach attire when shopping coastal areas
- **Cultural Sites**: Respect cultural significance when purchasing traditional items

### Transportation & Accessibility
- **Public Transit**: Many shopping areas accessible via trolley and bus system
- **Ride-Sharing**: Available throughout San Diego County
- **Bike-Friendly**: Many areas have bike parking and bike-friendly streets
- **ADA Accessibility**: Most shopping centers are wheelchair accessible
