11. **Walt Disney Concert Hall** - Architectural marvel by Frank Gehry
12. **Griffith Park** - One of the largest urban parks in the US
13. **Dodger Stadium** - Third oldest MLB park, historic venue
14. **Los Angeles County Museum of Art (LACMA)** - Premier art collections
15. **Runyon Canyon** - Popular hiking spot with city views and celebrity sightings
16. **Melrose Avenue** - Trendy shops, murals, and Instagram spots

18. **Olvera Street** - Historic birthplace of Los Angeles
